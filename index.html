Local NGOs

NGOs (Non-Governmental Organizations) work as advocates, educators, and action groups in the fight against marine pollution. They play a key role in raising awareness by educating communities about the impacts of marine waste on the environment, marine life, and human health.

Municipal Corporation

Municipal Corporations are responsible for managing waste at the local level. Their role in reducing marine waste is critical, as they oversee waste collection, segregation, recycling programs, and disposal. Proper waste management prevents waste from ending up in waterways that may eventually flow into oceans.

Recycling Companies

Recycling Companies are essential in processing waste to prevent it from reaching oceans and promoting the reuse of materials.These companies manage the collection, sorting, and processing of recyclable materials, diverting plastics, metals, and other waste from landfills and waterways.
